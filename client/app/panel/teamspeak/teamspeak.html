Your Access Token

You will be immediately moved into a channel called Unknown. This means that you have not yet linked your FACEIT Pro account and your Teamspeak profile yet.

Your token is {{auth.currentUser.tstoken}} - copy it.

The server will message you in the text chat asking for your token. Paste it into the chat and press enter.

If you cannot see the chats, click and drag the bottom line up to expand the text chats.

Using Teamspeak

Default Channels

Here is a list of the persistent TeamSpeak channels.


Lobby          - General player lounge and chat.

League lobbies - Each of the leagues. All players will be moved to their league channel after each game.

Lounge 1
Lounge 2       - General lounges to have meetings / chat.
Lounge 3

AFK            - Move here when you are away.
Unknown        - Initial channel.

id             - Channels for each of the current games. You will be moved into these channels automatically.

While in a game you will be automatically moved to a channel for the game.

Channels are created after the game starts, i.e, after everyone loads in.

Please do not join league channels unless you are in the respective league.
